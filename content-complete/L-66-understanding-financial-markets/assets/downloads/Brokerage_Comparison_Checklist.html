Next Steps

  1. Double-check account minimums - Make sure you can meet the minimum deposit requirement
  2. Read recent reviews - Check sites like NerdWallet, Investopedia, or Barron's for up-to-date reviews
  3. Test the platform - Many brokerages offer demo accounts; try before you commit
  4. Understand the fee structure - Read the fee schedule carefully to avoid surprises
  5. Check SIPC insurance - Ensure the brokerage is SIPC-insured (protects up to $500K)
