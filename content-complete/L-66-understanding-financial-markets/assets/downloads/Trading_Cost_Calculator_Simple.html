Understanding the Results:

  • Purchase Cost: What you pay to buy the shares (shares × ask price)
  • Immediate Market Value: What you'd receive if you sold immediately (shares × bid price)
  • Hidden Spread Cost: The difference - this is your immediate loss from the spread
  • Cost as % of Investment: How much the spread costs relative to your total purchase

💡 Key Insights:

  • Spreads < 0.1%: Very efficient, highly liquid stock
  • Spreads 0.1% - 0.5%: Acceptable for most stocks
  • Spreads > 0.5%: Warning! High cost, consider using a limit order
  • Spreads > 1%: Avoid or trade very carefully - extremely illiquid
⚠️ Remember: This spread cost happens every time you trade. Frequent trading multiplies these costs. For 10 round-trip trades, you'd pay this cost 20 times!
