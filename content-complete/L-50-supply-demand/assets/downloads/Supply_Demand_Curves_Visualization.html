How to Use This Tool:

  • Move the price slider to set different price levels
  • Watch the quantity demanded and supplied change as you move along the curves
  • Observe market conditions:
    • When price is above equilibrium: Surplus (quantity supplied > quantity demanded)
    • When price is below equilibrium: Shortage (quantity demanded > quantity supplied)
    • At equilibrium: Market clears (quantity demanded = quantity supplied)
  • Try different examples using the market selector buttons

Key Concepts:

Law of Demand: As price increases, quantity demanded decreases (downward-sloping curve)

Law of Supply: As price increases, quantity supplied increases (upward-sloping curve)

Equilibrium: The price where quantity demanded equals quantity supplied. At this point, the market "clears" and there is neither a surplus nor a shortage.
