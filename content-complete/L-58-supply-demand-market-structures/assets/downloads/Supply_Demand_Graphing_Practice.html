📝 Answer Key

Scenario 1: Smartphone Market - New Technology

Curve: Demand | Direction: Right (Increase)
Price: Increases | Quantity: Increases
Explanation: More consumers want the new technology, shifting demand right. Both P and Q rise.

Scenario 2: Coffee Market - Bad Harvest

Curve: Supply | Direction: Left (Decrease)
Price: Increases | Quantity: Decreases
Explanation: Less coffee available shifts supply left. Price rises, quantity falls.

Scenario 3: Streaming Services - Economic Recession

Curve: Demand | Direction: Left (Decrease)
Price: Decreases | Quantity: Decreases
Explanation: Fewer consumers willing to buy shifts demand left. Both P and Q fall.

Scenario 4: Electric Vehicles - New Factory Opens

Curve: Supply | Direction: Right (Increase)
Price: Decreases | Quantity: Increases
Explanation: More production capacity shifts supply right. Price falls, quantity rises.

Scenario 5: Concert Tickets - Sold-Out Tour

Curve: Demand | Direction: Right (Increase)
Price: Increases | Quantity: Stays constant (venue capacity limit)
Explanation: Massive demand increase, but supply is fixed. Price rises sharply, but quantity can't exceed seats available.

Scenario 6: Solar Panels - Government Subsidies

Curve: Supply | Direction: Right (Increase)
Price: Decreases | Quantity: Increases
Explanation: Lower production costs shift supply right. More panels at lower prices.

Scenario 7: Textbooks - Digital Alternatives

Curve: Demand | Direction: Left (Decrease)
Price: Decreases | Quantity: Decreases
Explanation: Substitute good reduces demand for printed books. Both P and Q fall.

Scenario 8: Housing Market - Construction Boom

Curve: Supply | Direction: Right (Increase)
Price: Decreases | Quantity: Increases
Explanation: More homes available shifts supply right. Prices moderate, more sales occur.
